Pong

"CLICK ONCE TO ENABLE GAME SOUND"
In order to play the game use the mouse and hover over
the player paddle to control its movement up and down the canvas.
First to reach 7 points wins the game.
The lightning power up indicates 2x faster speed for the ball.
The Plus sign power up indicates bigger paddle size.
Power ups appear at random positions.
Since the Bigger paddle Power up helps you win the game
a lot faster it only appears when the player seems to be losing!
Refresh the page in order to restart!
